Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our plans and services.

How do I get started?
Join our Discord server and open a ticket. Our team will respond within hours, discuss your needs, and guide you through the process from start to finish.
Can I upgrade my plan later?
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your hosting plan at any time. We'll prorate the remaining balance and apply it to your new plan.
What payment methods do you accept?
We currently accept PayPal and major credit/debit cards. Crypto payments are available on request for development services.
Do development quotes include hosting?
Development services are priced separately from hosting. However, we offer bundled discounts when you combine a development package with a hosting plan.
Is there a money-back guarantee?
Yes. All hosting plans come with a 7-day money-back guarantee. Development projects are handled with a milestone-based refund policy — see our Terms of Service for details.

Still Have Questions?

Our team is live in Discord. Drop a message and we'll help you choose the right plan.
